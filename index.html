General

  • Degeneration: Mental + Willpower vs. 10+Level of Sin

Combat

  • Initiative: Highest of Mental or Physical Attributes

  • Physical Attack: Physical + Melee/Brawl/Firearms + Bonus vs. Physical + Dodge

Mass Combat

  • Max Attacks Per Round
  • 2 Physical
  • 1 Social
  • 1 Mental
  • 2 Physical per Celerity Round (no mental/social).
